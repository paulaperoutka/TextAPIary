Welcome to Text APIary

Everyone can have their own personal online diary or journal on the Internet. It's free at Text APIary! The best part, is you can create it via text - with the convenience of your phone! We will host your journal online at no cost. Go ahead and create your own public or private diary now. You could even try out our group diaries if you have info you would like to share with your peers. If you have any suggestions or problems with this Text diary, please let us know.
